Dr. Data Decision Intelligence LLC builds private, air-gapped AI systems for knowledge workers: coaches, advisors, consultants, and clinicians who need the intelligence of AI without the compliance risk of cloud tools.

The Skill Threshold Zone framework was developed through a decade of workforce development and L&D practice, combined with hands-on AI systems architecture. It is the methodology behind every engagement, and the curriculum behind this workshop series.

The five-layer STZ system (Prompts, Skills, Agents, Contracts, Evaluation) is designed to be built once per client and maintained as a compounding asset. The workshop series makes the methodology accessible to any knowledge worker ready to design their position in the AI transition, rather than stumble into it.
