# Dr. Data Referral Trigger Cheat Sheet

## Listen For These Phrases

1. "I'm banning ChatGPT because of privacy concerns."
2. "We have thousands of Zoom recordings just sitting there."
3. "I need to prove to an auditor our AI is secure."
4. "Our data is scattered in five different places."
5. "We tried AI but it didn't work."
6. "I know we have valuable data but don't know how to use it."
7. "We're drowning in data but starving for insights."

## Ideal Client Profile

- **Industry:** Law, accounting, medical, consulting, agencies
- **Size:** 10-30 employees
- **Pain:** Data silos, compliance concerns, AI curiosity but no clarity
- **Budget:** $25K-$150K for initial engagement

## What to Say

> "I have a friend, Dr. Data, who builds Private AI Vaults. Unlike ChatGPT, her systems stay in your office and off the cloud. She can turn those messy Zoom calls and emails into a growth dashboard without leaking your secrets. Can I introduce you via email?"
